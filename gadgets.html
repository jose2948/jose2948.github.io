Devices

“sports trainers can measure and track performance in real time. Almost anything about the athlete can be measured, from breathing and heart rate, to hydration and temperature” (“How Technology Is Revolutionizing Sports Training”). These devices have really helped in the last few years which is something that couldn’t be done before. These sport training vests help with simple things, smaller things that humans aren’t able to pay attention to. Over the last few years people have used these vests to measure their performances and to improve.

Sensor wear for athletes

“Nowadays technical preparation has become much more easy and to also track any small mistakes. New wear sensor have helped a lot compared to back then where trainers would have to give feedback after practice or the athletes would videotape themselves “ (How Technology Is Revolutionizing Sports Training). This really helps when it comes to sports or running because it improves form and other mistakes such as the wrong footing of a runner or the body posture. Over the decades people would stay days trying to improve simple ways to improve their form when sprinting. But the new devices used for professionals have helped them a lot and could notice a small mistake right away.
